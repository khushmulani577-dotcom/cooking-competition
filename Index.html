Savoury Delights

Green Harmony Toast

Our Green Harmony Toast is made with semolina and topped with guacamole and hummus. Semolina gives slow-release energy and fiber, guacamole provides healthy fats and vitamin E, and hummus adds plant-based protein. Together, it is light, filling, and nourishing — a perfect balance of good carbs, protein, and healthy fats. ✨ A delicious bite of health and freshness.

Sweet Treats

Dates Delight

Nutrition Explanation (Short): Our Dates Delight – Guilt-Free Brownie is naturally sweetened with dates — no refined sugar, no maida. Oats, jowar, and wheat flour give slow-release carbs and high fiber, keeping you full longer. Dates add natural sweetness + iron, potassium, and antioxidants. Cocoa, hazelnuts, and almonds provide healthy fats and protein, good for skin and energy. The cashew cream cheese gives creaminess with plant-based protein. Fresh berries and seeds on top add vitamins, minerals, and omega-3.
